Artist bio
#

Crone Call is the dark-synth solo project of Portland’s Chloe Moon. Crone Call is disheartened music for the exhausted – inspired by coldwave, deathrock, NDW, and minimal synth.

Crone Call’s cried-out poetry warns listeners to act out and fashion a survival that works for them. The music is performed on a single analog synthesizer – with drum programming overlaid by gritty leads, uneasy strings, and expansive, warning-horns. The repetitive bass-lines drive the vibe, alternating between melodic punch and brooding growls.

DIY Super 8 films are shot and hand-developed in the cold comfort of her basement. With prisms, matte-paintings, and in-camera effects, the music videos draw inspiration from silent films, modernist paintings, and deep-seated fears about where life is carrying us.
